About me

I enjoy playing the drums, and my musical taste spans from jazz to rock. In 2020, I decided to face my greatest challenge: moving to Canada.

People around me often say I possess a contagious friendliness, an enthusiast for family ties, and laid-back gatherings with friends.

Despite missing family and friends, this journey of self-discovery has been enriching.

Studying in a country far from my origin not only fulfills a dream but also allows me to immerse myself in new experiences, expanding horizons and building friendships that transcend geographical borders.
